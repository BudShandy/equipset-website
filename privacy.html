Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 April 2026

EquipSet ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Irish and EU data protection law.

1. Who we are

EquipSet is a software-as-a-service platform for construction equipment safety inspections, operated by Techset Computers, Ireland. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at hello@equipset.app.

2. What data we collect

We may collect the following categories of personal data:

  • Account data — name, email address, company name, and password (stored as a secure hash) when you register for an account.
  • Usage data — information about how you use the platform, including inspection records, equipment data, site information, and audit logs.
  • Contact enquiries — your email address if you submit a contact or sign-up form on our marketing website.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited, collected automatically when you visit our website.
  • Cookies — see Section 6 below.

3. How we use your data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the EquipSet platform and your account.
  • To send you service-related communications such as account verification and password reset emails.
  • To respond to enquiries submitted via our website.
  • To improve and develop our platform based on usage patterns.
  • To comply with legal obligations, including health and safety record-keeping requirements.

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under GDPR:

  • Contract — processing necessary to provide the service you have signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our platform and prevent fraud.
  • Consent — for marketing communications and non-essential cookies, where you have opted in.
  • Legal obligation — where we are required to process data to comply with applicable law.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on Microsoft Azure infrastructure located in North Europe (Ireland). We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure, including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls.

We retain account data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, your data will be deleted within 90 days unless we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

6. Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the platform to function, including authentication tokens stored in your browser's local storage. These do not require consent.
  • Analytics cookies — used to understand how visitors use our website. We will only set these if you have given your consent via our cookie banner.

You can withdraw your consent to non-essential cookies at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or by clicking Decline on the cookie banner.

7. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services which may process your data:

  • Microsoft Azure — cloud hosting and storage (North Europe). Privacy policy.
  • SendGrid (Twilio) — transactional email delivery. Privacy policy.
  • Formspree — contact form submissions from our marketing website. Privacy policy.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

8. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access — you can request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — you can ask us to delete your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to restriction — you can ask us to restrict processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability — you can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — you can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@equipset.app. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Commission (DPC) Ireland at www.dataprotection.ie.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes by email. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at hello@equipset.app.
