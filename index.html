Features
Everything your site needs

From QR code scanning to compliance reporting — EquipSet covers the full equipment safety lifecycle.

QR-Powered Inspections

Scan any piece of equipment with a phone camera. Operators complete Pass/Fail checklists in under 60 seconds, with photo evidence and digital signature.

Compliance Tracking

Track Safe Pass certs, operator licences, and equipment certifications. Get alerts before they expire. Stay audit-ready at all times.

Automated Fault Actions

Failed inspection items automatically generate actions, assigned to the right person with priority levels. Nothing falls through the cracks.

Attendance & Clock-In

GPS-verified clock-in/out with geofencing. Know who's on site in real time. Exportable attendance records for payroll and HSA reporting.

Tool Talks & Documents

Upload safety briefings as PDFs. Operators read and sign off digitally. Site documents are organised by folder with access control.

Plant Hire Management

Track hire jobs end-to-end. Assign equipment, set rates per day or hour, record readings, and generate printable hire dockets instantly.
